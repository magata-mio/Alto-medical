How Alto works

Send in a prescription

Create an account to transfer your existing prescription or ask your doctor to send a new prescription to Alto.

See your savings

We'll search for savings on your medication and show you delivery options. We accept most major insurance plans.

Receive your delivery

You pick the day and time and we'll deliver it by hand, for free. No delivery or hidden fees.

It’s really that simple

Send us your prescription and we’ll take care of the rest. We handle your insurance paperwork and identify ways to save you money. As soon as your medication is ready, we’ll send a text or give you a call to schedule your free delivery.

The lowest price, no hidden fees

Our team works tirelessly behind-the-scenes to find you the best price. Every time you send us a prescription, we’ll comb through our database of coupons, copay cards, and other savings options. Plus, our services and delivery are always free — there are absolutely no hidden fees.

Free same-day delivery

We hand-deliver your medications at the time and location that works best for you. Get delivery updates via text or track your courier in real-time within the Alto app. We’ll leave your medications in a designated safe place for secure, contact-free delivery.

Chat with a pharmacist

When you have questions, our pharmacists are just a message away. No need to wait in line; simply call, text, or email whenever you need advice, 7 days a week.
